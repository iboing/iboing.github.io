Short Biography

Currently, I am a fourth-year graduate student (doctoral program) at Center for Data Science, Academy for Advanced Interdisciplinary Studies, Peking University. I am co-supervised by Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Dongmin Chen, and do researches in computer vision and machine learning. I received my bachelor’s degree of applied physics from Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology in 2016.

Looking for job and post-doc opportunities. If you are intrested, please contact me.

Research Interests

My research interests include CNN architectures, scene understanding, and learning based optimization.
